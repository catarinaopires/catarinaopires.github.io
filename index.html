    Hi! I'm Catarina Pires, a Master's student in Informatics and Computing Engineering at FEUP. Currently, I'm deeply immersed in my dissertation, which focuses on multimodal search, specifically integrating text and images. My research delves into the intersections of my favorite areas: information systems, artificial intelligence, and computer vision. Concurrently, I work as an Assistant Researcher at INESC TEC, where I direct my Master's thesis research efforts toward innovative applications in the medical field.


    I'm passionate about technology, constantly eager to learn new things and embrace new challenges.


    Beyond work, I enjoy spending time with friends, watching movies and TV series, and both playing and listening to music as a member of the philharmonic band "Banda da SFV".
